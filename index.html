Chessboard on the console ♞

Open your console 
        Window : Ctrl + Mayúscula + J
        MAC: Cmd + Opción + J

        A small example of styles in the Chrome console

        To move the piece indicate the initial position, followed by the final position

        Example: 

        Move white pawn to D4 -> d2d4 or D2D4

    
        Note:

        It is a proof of concept, does not validate the movements of the pieces, 
        nor does it have artificial intelligence.

        Tested in Chrome 63.
